Why choose GreenTwins ?

GreenTwins is a digital platform – SaaS or "On Premise" option – collaborative and intelligent offered to energy users and GHG emitters, in order to monitor, manage and optimize their energy efficiency (EE), improve their competitiveness and reduce their carbon and environmental footprint.

How does it work ?

For the operational needs of companies, GreenTwins digitalizes the energy performance professions/services based on AI and knowledge of energy performance profession energetics.

Partners

GreenTwins is aimed at the building sector, industry and networks of heat and cold. It provides customers with innovative AI algorithms aimed at improve and manage their energy and environmental performance, end-to-end and in real time.

Accompaniment

Our solution aims to make AI and energy efficiency businesses accessible to all companies wishing to improve their energy performance and environmental without having extensive expertise in data science and energetics.

Digital twins

The platform has energy and physical models of standard equipment, in the form of twins digital business and/or intelligent. They make it possible to monitor the level of performance of a equipment compared to the best technologies available, but also to detect inconsistencies on the input data and easily perform benchmarks.

GreenTwins is registered, “By Design” in a software eco-design and Green Cloud approach.

The project aims to deploy a feasibility study and R&D work, according to different shutters:

Step I

Feasibility study of a high performance software platform environmental, combining software engineering, data science and the profession of EE

Step II

Implementation of IoT systems and data transfer solutions reliable, less energy-consuming and eco-designed

Step III

R&D on the development of Big Data/AI solutions, responsible, efficient and minimizing resources (calculation, cloud)

Step IV

LCA on the global life cycle of the GreenTwins solution
